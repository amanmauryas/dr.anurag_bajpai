# About Dr. Anurag Bajpai

Dr. Anurag Bajpai is a nationally and internationally recognized Pediatric & Adolescent Endocrinologist, clinician-scientist, educator, and author. He currently serves as Consultant/Associate Director in Endocrinology at Regency Healthcare, Kanpur, while also leading MedEClasses, an internationally recognized educational platform dedicated to Pediatric Endocrinology.

After completing his MBBS and MD from the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi, Dr. Bajpai pursued advanced fellowship training in Pediatric Endocrinology at the Royal Children's Hospital, Melbourne, Australia, where he earned the FRACP qualification.

Over the past two decades, he has established himself as a pioneer in pediatric endocrine education, clinical research, and advanced endocrine care. His work spans patient care, guideline development, digital medical education, and international collaborations, making him one of the most respected names in Pediatric Endocrinology in India.
